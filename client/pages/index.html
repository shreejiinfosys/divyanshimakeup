Good to Know

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book for my wedding date?

For peak wedding season (Oct–Feb), we recommend reaching out 6–9 months ahead. Editorial and red-carpet requests can often be accommodated with shorter notice depending on travel schedules.

Do you travel outside Mumbai and Delhi?

Yes — destination weddings, international shoots, and out-of-city events are accepted regularly. Travel, stay, and on-location logistics are arranged as part of your proposal.

Is a trial session included?

Every bridal and major event booking includes one in-studio trial. This is where we finalize the look, test wear-time, and confirm products against your skin under your specific event lighting.

What does an inquiry cost, and how is pricing structured?

The consultation is complimentary. Final pricing is bespoke — built around event duration, location, team size, and product requirements — and shared as a personalised proposal after your inquiry.

Which products and brands are used?

Only professional, high-definition, long-wear formulations suited to camera and flash photography. Skin-specific and hypoallergenic alternatives are available on request — let us know during your consultation.
